 vision

we enhance public safety and promote successful community reintegration through education treatment and active participation in rehabilitative and restorative justice programs
 mission

to facilitate the successful reintegration of the in the individuals in our care back to their communities equipped with the tools to be drugfree healthy and employable members of society by providing education treatment rehabilitative and restorative justice programs all in a safe and humane environment
